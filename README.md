# Hybrid Renewable Charging Station

# Overview

The Hybrid Renewable Charging Station is a sustainable energy solution that integrates multiple renewable energy sources, such as solar and wind power, to provide reliable charging for electric vehicles and portable electronic devices. The system is designed to reduce dependence on conventional electricity while promoting clean energy utilization.

# Objectives

1. Develop an eco-friendly charging station using renewable energy.
* Improve energy efficiency through hybrid power generation.
* Promote sustainable transportation and reduce carbon emissions.
* Provide an uninterrupted power supply by combining multiple renewable sources.

# Features

* Hybrid solar and wind energy generation.
* Battery energy storage system.
* Automatic power management.
* Eco-friendly and cost-effective design.
* Suitable for EV charging and portable electronic devices.

# Technologies and Components

* Solar Panels
* Wind Turbine
* Rechargeable Battery
* Charge Controller
* DC-DC Converter
* Inverter
* Microcontroller (if applicable)
* Voltage and Current Sensors

# Working Principle

The solar panels and wind turbine generate electrical energy, which is regulated using a charge controller and stored in a rechargeable battery. The stored energy is then supplied to charging ports through suitable power conversion circuits. The hybrid system ensures continuous power availability even when one renewable source is unavailable.
